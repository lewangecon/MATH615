Week 9: 10/16 - 10/22

Last Updated 2017-10-15 20:45:06

Tuesday: Categorical Data Analysis

Goals:

  • Explain the concept of “deviation away from expected” and how that pertains to testing equal proportions.
  • Conduct a \(\chi^2\) squared test of equal proportions.

Prepare

  • Watch Passion Driven Statistics [Chapter 12] Video.
  • Review Lec 04 - Categorical data analysis [HTML] or [PDF] notes.

In class

  • Short lecture on construction of Chi-squared test statistic and necessary assumptions (skim through of lecture notes).
  • Work on Bivarite inference assignment.

Resources

  • [OpenIntro Statistics] Chapter 6 (6.1-6.4)
  • UCLA IDRE pages: \(\chi^2\) squared test in [SPSS] or [R]
  • Mike Marin: Relative Risk and Odds Ratio https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V_YNPQoAyCc
  • An excellent additional textbook reference: Categorical Data Analysis by Alan Agresti

Thursday: Correlation & Regression

Goals:

  • Conduct a correlation analyses in your software program of choice. Understand how to read the output.
  • Describe the relationship between an independent variable X and a continuous dependent variable \(Y\) as a straight line. The textbook discusses two cases:
    • Fixed-\(X\): values of \(X\) are preselected by investigator
    • Variable-\(X\): have random sample of \((X,Y)\) values
    • Calculations are the same, don’t stress too much about the difference.
  • Draw inferences regarding this relationship

Prepare

  • Read PMA6 Chapter 6
  • Review Lec 05 - Linear Regression [HTML] or [PDF] notes.

In class

  • Short lecture on linear regression analysis (quick overview of lecture notes)
  • Work on Bivariate inference assignment.

Resources

UCLA IDRE pages:

  • Correlation in [SPSS] or [R]
  • Linear Regression in [SPSS] or [R]
