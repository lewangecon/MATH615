Tuesday: Probability

Goals:

  • Give an example of a random variable
  • Calculate a probability under a normal model.
  • Describe how a sample statistic can estimate a population parameter
  • Describe the difference between the standard deviation, and the standard error.

Prepare

  • At minimum watch the PDS video on probability. If you are constrained for time you can pause when they really launch into hypothesis testing.
